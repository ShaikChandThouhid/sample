Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our SAP solutions, enterprise integration, support services, and digital transformation capabilities.

What industries do you specialize in for SAP implementation?

We work with a wide range of industries including manufacturing, logistics, retail, healthcare, and government sectors. Our consultants tailor each SAP solution to meet your industry-specific compliance, workflow, and performance needs.

How long does it take to implement an SAP solution?

The implementation timeline depends on the project scope, modules, and integration complexity. On average, a typical SAP rollout ranges from 3 to 6 months. With our rapid deployment accelerators, we help you go live faster.

Can Tekharkhe integrate SAP with our existing systems?

Yes. We specialize in system integration and can connect SAP with CRM platforms, accounting software, inventory systems, HRMS, and third-party APIs to ensure seamless data flow and process automation.

Do you provide post-go-live SAP support?

Absolutely. Tekharkhe offers 24/7 support and managed SAP services — including incident management, performance optimization, upgrades, and security monitoring.

What makes Tekharkhe different from other SAP service providers?

We combine deep technical expertise with a strong focus on innovation. Our clients choose us for our client-centric approach, faster go-to-market delivery, and ability to scale solutions globally.

Do you provide SAP training for internal teams?

Yes. We offer customized training programs for end-users, IT staff, and executives — ensuring successful adoption, reduced resistance, and improved ROI on your SAP investment.

Can Tekharkhe help us migrate from legacy systems to SAP?

Definitely. We specialize in legacy system modernization, helping companies migrate from outdated ERPs or manual systems to SAP S/4HANA or cloud-based SAP platforms with minimal disruption.

Is cloud-based SAP deployment an option?

Yes. We support cloud, on-premise, and hybrid SAP deployments. Based on your goals and infrastructure, we recommend the best-fit architecture for scalability, security, and cost-efficiency.

What is the difference between SAP ECC and SAP S/4HANA?

SAP ECC is the older ERP suite, while SAP S/4HANA is the next-generation platform built on an in-memory database for real-time data processing. We help businesses migrate to S/4HANA to improve speed, performance, and usability.

Does Tekharkhe offer custom SAP module development?

Yes. Our development team can custom-build SAP modules or enhancements to fit your business processes, whether it's custom reporting, additional fields, or tailored workflows.

Can you help us with SAP data migration?

Absolutely. We follow a secure, phased data migration process that includes data cleansing, validation, and testing — ensuring minimal disruption and maximum accuracy.

Do you work with cloud platforms like AWS or Azure?

Yes. There are no restrictions on repatriating profits, Yes. We’re experienced in deploying SAP on AWS, Microsoft Azure, and private cloud environments. We also assist with cloud strategy, security setup, and cost optimization.
